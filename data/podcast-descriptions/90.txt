Self-Discover is a fascinating new algorithm from researchers at Google DeepMind and USC that searches for "atomic reasoning modules". One of the quickest ways to improve your LLM programs is to add Chain-of-Thought, "Let's think step by step ...", another primitive like this could be "Let's think out of the box" or "Think symbolically to calculate an answer...".  Self-Discover searches for these thinking primitives that work best for YOUR LLM programs!!
